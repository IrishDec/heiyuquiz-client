Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about using HeiyuQuiz.

What is HeiyuQuiz?

HeiyuQuiz is an online quiz platform that allows users to create and play quizzes on a wide range of topics. It is designed to be simple, fast, and accessible without requiring account creation.

Do I need to sign up to use HeiyuQuiz?

No. You can generate and play quizzes without creating an account or providing personal information. This keeps the experience quick and accessible for everyone.

What topics can I create quizzes about?

HeiyuQuiz supports many topics, including Anime, Science, History, Geography, Movies, and General Knowledge. You can also enter custom topics to tailor quizzes to your interests.

Are the quiz questions reused?

No. Each quiz is generated dynamically, meaning questions are not pulled from a fixed list. This keeps quizzes fresh and reduces repetition, even when playing the same topic multiple times.

Is HeiyuQuiz suitable for classrooms?

Yes. Teachers often use HeiyuQuiz for revision, warm-up activities, and informal assessment. Because no login is required and it works in a browser, it can be used easily in classrooms or remote learning environments.

Can I use HeiyuQuiz for group games or trivia nights?

Absolutely. HeiyuQuiz works well for group play, whether in person or online. Quiz links can be shared easily, and time-based scoring adds friendly competition.

Does HeiyuQuiz work on mobile devices?

Yes. HeiyuQuiz is mobile-friendly and works on phones, tablets, and desktops using a modern web browser. No downloads or installations are required.

Is HeiyuQuiz free to use?

HeiyuQuiz is free to use. Some features or improvements may be introduced in the future, but core quiz creation and play are available without charge.

Does HeiyuQuiz collect personal data?

HeiyuQuiz is designed to minimise data collection. You can play quizzes without creating an account. Any limited data used for site functionality is described in the Privacy Policy.

How does scoring work?

Scores are based on correct answers and response time. Faster correct answers receive higher scores, which encourages quick thinking without penalising accuracy.

Why doesn’t HeiyuQuiz require an account?

Removing account requirements reduces friction and makes the platform more accessible. Users can start playing immediately without sharing personal details.

Can I suggest new features or report issues?

Yes. Feedback helps improve HeiyuQuiz. Feature ideas and bug reports can be submitted through the contact information listed on the site.

Is HeiyuQuiz suitable for all ages?

HeiyuQuiz is intended for general audiences. Quiz content depends on the chosen topic, so supervision may be appropriate for younger users depending on subject matter.

Will HeiyuQuiz add more categories?

Additional categories and improvements are planned over time. The goal is to expand quiz options while keeping the platform simple and reliable.
