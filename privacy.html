Privacy Policy Last updated: 18 Sep 2025

We care about your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you use HeiyuQuiz (the “Service”), why we collect it, and how we handle it. If you have questions, email us at support@heiyuquiz.com.

Who we are

Controller: HeiyuQuiz (Ireland). Contact: support@heiyuquiz.com.

What we collect

  • Quiz submissions: the name you enter, your selected answers (“picks”), score and timestamps.
  • Device/network data: basic technical logs (IP address, user-agent) for security and rate-limiting.
  • Cookies & local storage: we store lightweight preferences (e.g., your name, country, UI choices) in your browser. You can clear these anytime.
  • Ads & measurement: we use Google AdSense which may set cookies or similar identifiers to serve and measure ads. See Google’s Advertising Policy.

Why we collect it (lawful bases)

  • Provide the Service (contract / legitimate interests): create quizzes, accept answers, show results.
  • Security & abuse prevention (legitimate interests): rate-limits, combat spam/fraud.
  • Ads (consent/legitimate interests depending on your region): show and measure advertising.

Where your data lives

  • Application database: We use Supabase to store quizzes and submissions. Only the data we need is saved (quiz id, name, score, picks, timestamps).
  • AI question generation: We use OpenAI to generate some quiz questions. We avoid sending personal data in prompts. See OpenAI’s policies.
  • Ads: Google may process data as described in their policy linked above.

How long we keep it

Quiz submissions are kept so players can view results after the game. We typically retain raw submissions for up to 12 months unless you request deletion earlier or we need to keep them longer for legal reasons. Aggregated, non-identifiable stats may be kept longer.

Your choices

  • Consent banner: you can accept or reject optional cookies where required.
  • Browser controls: clear cookies/localStorage to remove stored preferences on your device.
  • Access / deletion: email support@heiyuquiz.com with the quiz link and the name you used. We’ll locate and delete your submission, subject to verification.
  • Ad choices: manage Google ad settings at adssettings.google.com.

International transfers

We may process data outside your country. When we do, we rely on lawful transfer mechanisms (e.g., EU Standard Contractual Clauses implemented by our providers).

Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child under 13 has used the Service, contact us to remove the data.

Do Not Sell / Share (CCPA/CPRA)

We don’t sell personal information. Ad partners may be considered “sharing” under CPRA for cross-context behavioral advertising; you can opt-out where offered or by using browser signals.

Changes

If we make material changes, we’ll update the “Last updated” date and, when appropriate, provide additional notice.

Contact

Email: support@heiyuquiz.com
Address: HeiyuQuiz, Dublin, Ireland (update if different).
