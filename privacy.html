Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HeiyuQuiz collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and services.

1. Overview

HeiyuQuiz is designed to be usable without creating an account or providing personal details. Our goal is to minimise data collection while still delivering a smooth quiz experience.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require users to register or submit personal information to play quizzes. However, limited data may be processed automatically to support site functionality.

  • Usage data: Anonymous information such as page views, device type, and general usage patterns may be collected for performance and analytics purposes.
  • Local storage data: The site uses browser local storage to remember quiz-related preferences such as nickname, selected country, and quiz progress. This data stays on your device.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

HeiyuQuiz uses cookies and browser storage technologies to ensure the site works correctly and to improve user experience.

Functional storage is required for core features such as quiz completion and result display. Optional cookies may be used for analytics or advertising, where applicable.

Full details are available in our Cookie Policy.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

When enabled, third-party advertising partners such as Google AdSense may use cookies or similar technologies to display ads and measure performance.

These partners operate under their own privacy policies, and HeiyuQuiz does not control how third-party cookies are used.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Information is only shared where required to operate the site, comply with legal obligations, or support analytics and advertising services.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect information processed through the site. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

7. User Choices

You can control cookies and local storage through your browser settings. You may also reset cookie preferences using controls provided on the site.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in functionality, legal requirements, or services. The latest version will always be available on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how data is handled, please contact us through the website’s contact form.
